Our
Programs
We believe in making a difference
through education by providing
holistic programming that can help
individuals build beneficial life skills
and values.
MENTORSHIP
The MYPI Mentorship program
supports youth in their academic,
religious, and personal lives.
MENTAL WELLNESS
This program will turn MYPI into an
accessible and trusted network for youth
mental health in the Muslim community.
PATHWAYS TO SUCCESS
The Pathways to Success program equips
youth with skills and supports for college
readiness and career success.
ADOPT-A-FAMILY
The Adopt-A-Family turns youth into
ambassadors who support and serve
refugee families.
2020 CENSUS AMBAS-
SADORS PROGRAM
A past program, the 2020 Census Project,
one of our most successful initiatives yet,
amplified the voices of Muslims,
immigrants, refugees, people of color, and
people of faith by including them in the US
Census.
